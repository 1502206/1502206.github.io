Researching CPUs can be a time consuming process that involves scrounging for info from around the web and flipping between tons of browser tabs to compare specs. CPUBoss is useful because it captures a massive amount of specs, benchmarks, and reviews data, and organizes it in one central place as a one-stop-shop for CPU research
